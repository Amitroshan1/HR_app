SAFFO SOLUTION TECHNOLOGY

Domestic Travel Policy

Purpose

• The purpose of travel policy is to ensure that the travel expense of the company is controlled by establishing certain standards.

• Employees must control business travel expense by making the sound judgment with respect to use of company funds.

• Travel is an important aspect for carrying out the business; it is expected from employees that they will follow the “travel guidelines for employees” while travelling and would provide documentation in support of the same.

• It is also meant to reimburse the amount spent by an employee when on travel.

General guidelines to be followed in the policy

1. An email needs to be sent to the accounts department along with approval mail to get the travel advance.

2. No expense will be reimbursed for alcoholic beverages or cigarette etc.

3. In case an employee wants to combine business stay with personal vacations, it must be duly approved by the Project Manager/Team Lead. It should be noted that no reimbursement would happen for any personal expense.

4. All engineers are required to properly declare all hand carried items including technical data with Project Manager and Team Lead.

5. Kindly carry items only relating to project’s test scope.

6. Employees are required to report airport as per the below terms:
• For Domestic tour - employee need to report 2 hours early prior to their flight time and 1.5 hours early prior to their train time.
• All engineers are requested to complete their check-in and boarding procedures on time, while departure and in transit.

7. In case flight/train missed by employee without any valid reason, the amount will be recovered from employee salary.

8. The employee is not eligible to take any leave if the FT is up to 15 days.

9. If the FT is for 30 days, the employee is entitled for 2 days of leave.

10. Leave can be allowed to an employee on tour on a case-to-case basis by the Team Lead. No other charges/DA will be admissible for such period of leave.

11. If 2 or more employees are travelling to the same location, they are advised to share the ride.

12. No employee is allowed to carry any medicines for self/family/friends/colleagues/relatives. In case require any medicines for self, need to have original prescription for medicines is mandatory. Quantity of medicines should not exceed the dosage requirements for business trip and this also need to declare with Team Lead and Project Manager on email.

13. Do not hand carry any items of unknown person during your travel.

14. Do not discuss or disclose the work type with anybody during your travel.

15. While travelling in Bus or train, avoid communicating with strangers, and avoid sharing work related information with strangers.

16. Do not show prototypes to outsiders without business reasons.

17. Avoid using and handling prototypes during your leisure time, after office hours.

18. Restrict yourself from consuming any substance that can lower your alertness (examples are like alcohol, drugs, tobacco etc.)

19. Saffo Solution Technology will not be held responsible for any customs/policies/judicial enquiries relating to activities which are out of scope of test services for e.g. Custom enquiries for carrying medicines or gifts for friends or relatives or friends’ relatives.

20. Employees are strictly forbidden from sharing corporate travel itineraries on social media sites including Facebook and LinkedIn or any other social media sites.

21. Avoid posting any "reviews" or comments about prototypes/devices on social networking sites like Facebook, Twitter and Trip Advisor or any social media sites.

22. No reimbursement will be made, if anyone books hotel by his own without prior approval from travel department over the email.

23. You are also requested to share the cab details used at any location.

24. For every sim purchase or recharge needed, prior approval on mail is need from Management.

Expense Claim Submission Process

1. Request for Travel expenses will be submitted in prescribed format and approved by the competent authority.

2. Expense claim to be submitted within 72 hours on return from tour with approval of your Project Manager.

3. All Expenses incurred during 1st to 15th of a month will be reimbursed on 20th of the month. (Subject to all supporting documents submitted with approved claim).

4. All Expenses incurred during 16th to 30th will be reimbursed on 05th of the following month. (Subject to all supporting documents submitted with approved claim).

5. All claims MUST be supported by printed vouchers/receipts and other relevant documents. In case of no supporting or handwritten receipt, you should provide details of car driver or shop keeper mobile number. Also, relevant withdrawal slip is must. Importantly, we need to have receipt for every expense.

6. Kindly rename the scan copy/pics of bills according to the heading of the expense. (E.g. – In expense claim, expense is mention as “Purchase of car Inverter” the receipt of car inverter also renames as “Purchase of car Inverter “).

7. Scan or take snap of Bill which should be clear and visible before sending along with the expense claim. Make sure the purpose of expense, date and amount clearly visible before submitting supporting documents.

8. Take bill of each and every thing purchased for Official purpose.

9. Claims scrutiny will be done and any discrepancy will be informed by email. Claim will be reimbursed after completion of all scrutiny and checking.

10. Any Travel expense not been used by employee on travel, such amount shall be returned to accounts department with a confirmation mail.

11. All reimbursement bills must be supported by original bills / receipts etc.

12. Any arrangement made over and above the eligibility criterion the same will be deducted from employee salary.

13. If any company assets are lost while on tour the same should be informed to the concerned authority immediately. In case no action taken company reserves the right to recover the amount of lost assets from employee salary.

14. Most economical preferably local transport must be availed for ground transportation.

Following expenses will be reimbursed if incurred for official purpose

1. DA for Domestic Trip :- A) DA for Domestic Trip less than 2 weeks = INR 700/- per day
B) DA will be 14000 INR per month if engineer staying outside than his base location for one month. Excess days after 1 month will be calculated on pro-rata basis.
C) DA for trip more than 2 weeks will be calculated on Pro-rata basis. (E.g : - If Engineer stay 22 days outside base location. He will get DA as 14000/30*22 = INR 10267. Here we assume as 30-day month)

2. While travelling Rs. 350 per day will be applicable.

3. Your DA will start after reaching the Test location, and will stop after leaving the test location.

4. SIM Purchase, Recharge and bill payment. (Must activate plan according to the testing requirement).

5. Cab for Mobility (Inform per day charges to your immediate manager for approval via email before or after using the cab for one day).

6. Hotel Payment

7. Train/Air Ticket Payment

8. Airport Tax

9. Printing and Stationery

10. Airport Pick up & Drop (Hire Only small cabs) & local public transport where ever is possible by keeping safety of Devices.

11. Purchases Related to project. (Need specific approval from Lead on Email.)

Any expenses other than above will not be reimbursed except specific approval from project leads or manager for each expense with amount incurred or estimated on email.

We may any time revise the policy and will inform in advance. It is strictly requested engineer to abide with the travel policy.

Breach of the above terms will lead to termination of services.
