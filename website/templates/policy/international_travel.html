SAFFO SOLUTION TECHNOLOGY

International Travel Policy

Purpose

• The Purpose of travel policy is to ensure that the travel expense of the company is controlled by establishing certain standards.

• Employees must control business travel expense by making the sound judgment with respect to use of company funds.

• Travel is an important aspect for carrying out the business, it is expected from employees that they will follow the “travel guidelines for employees” while travelling and would provide documentation in support of the same.

• It is also meant to reimburse the amount spent by an employee when on international or domestic travel.

General guidelines to be followed in the policy

1. An email needs to be sent to the accounts department along with approval mail to get the travel advance.

2. No expense will be reimbursed for alcoholic beverages or cigarette etc.

3. In case an employee wants to combine business stay with personal vacations, it must be duly approved by the Project Manager/Team Lead. It should be noted that no reimbursement would happen for any personal expense.

4. All engineers are required to properly declare all hand carried items including technical data with Project Manager and Travel Team.

5. Kindly carry items only relating to project’s test scope.

6. Employees are required to report airport as per the below terms:
• For International tour – employee need to report 3 hours early prior to their flight time
• All engineers are requested to complete their check in and boarding procedures on time, while departure and in transit.

7. In case flight/train missed by employee without any valid reason, the amount will be recovered from employee salary.

8. The employee is not eligible to take any leave if the FT is up to 15 days.

9. If the FT is for 30 days, the employee is entitled for 2 days of leave.

10. Leave can be allowed to an employee on tour on a case-to-case basis by the Team Lead. No other charges/DA will be admissible for such period of leave.

11. If 2 or more employees are travelling to the same location, they are advised to share the ride.

12. No employee is allowed to carry any medicines for self/family/friends/colleagues/relatives. In case require any medicines for self, it is mandatory to have original prescription for the medicines. Quantity of medicines should not exceed the dosage requirements for business trip and this also need to declare with travel team and Project Manager on email.

13. It is advisable not to carry any personal gift items from India for friends or relatives residing abroad or purchase it in respective country and carry it back while returning to your destination country.

14. Do not hand carry any items of unknown person during your travel (International/Domestic).

15. Do not discuss or disclose the work type with anybody during your travel.

16. While travelling in Bus or train, avoid communicating with strangers, and avoid sharing work related information with strangers.

17. Do not show prototypes to outsiders without business reasons.

18. Avoid using and handling prototypes during your leisure time, after office hours.

19. Restrict yourself from consuming any substance that can lower your alertness (examples are like alcohol, drugs, tobacco etc.)

20. Saffo Solution Technology will not be held responsible for any customs/policies/judicial enquiries relating to activities which are out of scope of test services for e.g. Custom enquiries for carrying medicines or gifts for friends or relatives or friend’s relatives.

21. Employees are strictly forbidden from sharing corporate travel itineraries on social media sites including Facebook and LinkedIn or any other social media sites.

22. Avoid posting any "reviews" or comments about prototypes/devices on social networking sites like Facebook, Twitter and Trip Advisor or any social media sites.

23. No reimbursement will be made, if anyone books hotel by his own without prior approval from travel department over the email.

24. You are also requested to share the cab details used at any location.

25. For every sim purchase or recharge needed, prior approval on mail is need from Management.

26. Only 3 ATM withdrawals are allowed for 15 days, take the project manager’s approval on mail if more transactions are required. In case the project is extended for more than 15 days the transactions will be allowed by the Project Manager and Accounts Team as per the project requirement.

Expense Claim Submission Process

1. Request for Travel expenses will be submitted in prescribed format and approved by the competent authority.

2. Expense claim to be submitted within 72 hours on return from tour with approval of your Project Manager.

3. All Expenses incurred during 1st to 15th of a month will be reimbursed on 20th of the month.
(Subject to all supporting documents submitted with approved claim).

4. All Expenses incurred during 16th to 30th will be reimbursed on 05th of the following month.
(Subject to all supporting documents submitted with approved claim).

5. All claims MUST be supported by printed vouchers/receipts and other relevant documents. In case of no supporting or handwritten receipt, you should provide details of car driver or shop keeper mobile number. Also, relevant withdrawal slip is must. Importantly, we need to have receipt for every expense.

6. Kindly rename the scan copy/pics of bills according to the heading of the expense. (E.g. – In expense claim, expense is mention as “Purchase of car Inverter” the receipt of car inverter also rename as “Purchase of car Inverter“).

7. Scan or take snap of Bill which should be clear and visible before sending along with the expense claim. Make sure the purpose of expense, date and amount clearly visible before submitting supporting documents.

8. Take bill of each and every thing purchased for Official purpose.

9. Claims scrutiny will be done and any discrepancy will be informed by email. Claim will be reimbursed after completion of all scrutiny and checking.

10. Please send the bank account statement and Credit Card statement as well along with travel card statement while submitting any International & Domestic Claim.

11. Provide bank ATM withdrawal slip for each international withdrawal.

12. Any travel expense not been used by employee on travel, such amount shall be returned to accounts department with a confirmation mail.

13. All reimbursement bills must be supported by original bills / receipts etc.

14. Any arrangement made over and above the eligibility criterion the same will be deducted from employee salary.

15. If any company assets are lost while on tour the same should be informed to the concerned authority immediately. In case no action taken, company reserves the right to recover the amount of lost assets from employee salary.

16. All payments while on international travel can be made by credit card/debit card, the amount can be reimbursed on submission of actual. The points however earned by use of credit card can be used by an employee on personal use.

17. All claims which are made by foreign exchange / foreign currency can be settled with accounts department on submission of actual, upon return from travel. However, all foreign exchange must be drawn from an authorized agent. It is as per business travel policy.

18. Most economical preferably local transport must be availed for ground transportation.

FOLLOWING EXPENSES WILL BE REIMBURSED IF INCURRED FOR OFFICIAL PURPOSE

1. DA for International Trip :-

A) DA for International Trip is $50 per day for USA and Europe, and for rest of the world is $40 per day.

B) DA for engineers having stay one month or more than one month will be $1000 per month and will be calculated on Pro-rata basis for extra days after one month.

C) DA for trip more than 2 weeks will be calculated on Pro-rata basis. (Eg.- If engineer stays 22 days outside base location, he will get DA as 1000$/30*22 = $733. Here we assume 30 days a month)

2. While travelling no DA will be applicable.

3. SIM Purchase, Recharge and bill payment. (Must activate plan according to the testing requirement)

4. Cab for Mobility (Check with Travel team for cab driver details before going for the trip. If not provided, inform per day charges to the accounts@saffotech.com and immediate Reporting Manager for approval by email before or after using the cab for one day).

5. Hotel Payment

6. Air Ticket Payment

7. Airport Tax

8. Printing and Stationery

9. Airport Pick up & Drop (Hire only small cabs) & local public transport wherever possible by keeping safety of devices.

10. Purchases related to project. (Need specific approval from Reporting Manager on Email.)

Note: Any expenses other than above will not be reimbursed except specific approval from project leads or manager for each expense with amount incurred or estimated on Email.

We may any time revise the policy and will inform in advance. It is strictly requested engineer to abide with the travel policy.

Breach of the above terms will lead to termination of services.
